## Notes Per Border

### Product Truth -> Territory Truth

- What is clear internally but not yet proven in the market?
- Where are we optimizing copy before mapping topology?

### Penetration -> Sales

- Where is belief landing without commitment?
- Which trust layer is missing?

### Sales -> Retention

- What promise is not being matched by first experience?
- Where does silence or overwhelm appear?

### Retention -> Referral

- Are we producing satisfaction or advocacy?
- Where is public proof still weak?

### Referral -> Penetration

- Are referrals crossing weak ties?
- Are shared assets strong enough to enter new clusters?
