# Cohort As Product

## Definition

In K2M, the cohort is not just the delivery container. The cohort itself is part of the product: the social surface, witness structure, pacing, proof loops, and shared identity movement are part of what the learner buys.

## Pattern

This product logic treats collective structure as value:

- the 8-week arc gives enough time for identity and habit movement
- the group makes private struggle legible as a shared pattern
- community visibility creates witnesses, accountability, and proof
- artifacts become public evidence that the change was real
- Discord is valuable because it hosts the cohort body, not just the content

The result is that product quality cannot be judged only by curriculum quality or bot quality. It also depends on whether the cohort itself produces safety, movement, and visible transformation.

## Diagnostic Use

Ask:

- Does this change strengthen the cohort as a social product, or only add content?
- Are students getting witnesses, accountability, and proof, or just instructions?
- If the same curriculum were delivered alone, would the value drop?

## Trap

The trap is treating the cohort as a neutral wrapper around lessons. That collapses the social architecture into logistics and makes the product look thinner than it really is.
