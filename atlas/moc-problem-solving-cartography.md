# MOC: Problem-Solving Cartography

> Use this map to read hard problems as systems of framing, causality, constraint, uncertainty, trust, and redesign rather than as isolated symptoms waiting for techniques.

## How To Use This Map
Start here when the visible problem is loud but the governing structure is still unclear. Use it to separate symptom from cause, choose method by terrain, and decide whether the real move is to fix, redesign, or dissolve the problem.

## What This Map Reveals
Problem solving is not mainly a bag of tools. It is the ability to see what kind of problem exists, what force is governing the outcome, what representation is distorting diagnosis, and what intervention logic is actually plausible.
