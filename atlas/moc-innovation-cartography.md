# MOC: Innovation Cartography

> Use this map to read innovation as a trust-and-timing problem grounded in real demand, optional search, diffusion, institutional shelter, and industrialization rather than as novelty theater.

## How To Use This Map
Start here when a team is mistaking capability for demand, hype for timing, pilots for durable systems, or internal permission for real-world adoption.

## What This Map Reveals
Innovation survives when it resolves real problem tension, emerges from the adjacent possible at the right window, diffuses through trust, survives organizational antibodies, and crosses the industrialization gap.
