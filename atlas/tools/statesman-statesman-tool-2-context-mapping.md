# Statesman Tool 2: Context Mapping

> Applied to Kenyan Politics

## Purpose
Applied to Kenyan Politics

## Process
- For any political action, map all the contexts where it will be interpreted
- Your rural constituency interprets differently than Nairobi media
- Your party leadership interprets differently than your youth base
- Design actions that create favorable interpretations across critical contexts
- To parents: "Your child's competitive edge"
- To national media: "Innovation MP transforming education"
- To party leadership: "Constituency development model"
- To youth: "One of us actually delivering change"
