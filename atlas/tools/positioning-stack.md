# Positioning Stack

> If the team cannot say the frame clearly, the market will say it badly.

## Purpose
Use the positioning stack to make the product's comparison frame and value proof explicit before the market improvises it for you.

## Process
Write down:
1. the competitive alternatives
2. the differentiated capability
3. the specific audience
4. the category frame
5. the value proof

## Result
- category confusion becomes easier to diagnose
- product and market language get tighter
- the team can test whether framing is carrying or obscuring the product

## When To Use
Use this when strong capability is failing to become legible demand.

## Failure Mode
Writing elegant positioning that the product still cannot prove.
