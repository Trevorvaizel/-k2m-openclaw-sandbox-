# Tool 5: Explanation Acceleration

> *"If you can't explain it to a 6-year-old, you don't understand it." â€” Feynman*

## The Process
1. Learn something
2. Immediately explain it to a beginner
3. Notice where you **can't** explain simply
4. Those gaps = what you don't actually understand
5. Go back and learn those gaps

## The Result
"I understood it" vs "I can explain it simply" = massive gap. Teaching reveals the gaps. Filling gaps = real understanding.

## Force Connections
- The bridge from [[stage-4-unconscious-competence|Stage 4]] to [[stage-5-conscious-mastery|Stage 5]]
- Tests [[context-spectrum|Force 6]]: can you explain in multiple contexts?
- Reveals [[beginners-mind|Force 4]]'s cycle: teaching forces you back to beginner perspective

## Cross-Domain Example
**This entire project:** Klause extracting atomic notes from manifestos is Explanation Acceleration at scale. Every note that's hard to write reveals a gap in understanding. Every cross-domain connection that's hard to make reveals a missing lattice link.
