# AI Eval Card

> If capability, safety, latency, cost, and fallback are not explicit, the team is shipping vibes.

## Purpose
Use the AI eval card to make ship-readiness explicit for AI products before demos, excitement, or executive pressure outrun trustworthy evaluation.

## Process
Define:
1. capability thresholds
2. safety thresholds
3. latency limits
4. cost limits
5. UX fallbacks
6. who has authority to block shipment

## Result
- AI quality becomes discussable in operational terms
- teams stop mistaking demos for readiness
- product judgment gains a concrete evaluation surface

## When To Use
Use this when an AI feature looks impressive but trust, cost, or safety still feel hand-waved.

## Failure Mode
Using the card as a checkbox artifact instead of a real gating mechanism.
