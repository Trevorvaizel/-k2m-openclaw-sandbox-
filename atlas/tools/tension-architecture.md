# Tension Architecture

> Map what the audience knows, fears, suspects, and is being denied.

## Purpose
Use tension architecture to make narrative pressure explicit instead of relying on instinct alone.

## Process
Map:
1. what the audience knows
2. what they need to know
3. what they fear
4. what they falsely assume
5. what you are deliberately delaying

## Result
- withholding becomes intentional
- release timing becomes easier to tune
- flat scenes reveal missing pressure variables

## When To Use
Use this when the story contains events but does not feel alive.

## Failure Mode
Treating tension as only suspense. Tension can be emotional, moral, social, or cognitive.
