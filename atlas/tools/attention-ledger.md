# Attention Ledger

> Map the bargain before you optimize the metric.

## Purpose
Use the attention ledger to make the attentional bargain explicit: who is asking for attention, from whom, under what condition, and for what declared and hidden purpose.

## Process
Record:
1. who is asking for attention
2. from whom
3. at what moment
4. under what cognitive condition
5. for what declared purpose
6. for what hidden incentive
7. what the user receives
8. what the system receives

## Result
- hidden asymmetries surface earlier
- attentional extraction becomes easier to spot
- product teams can compare user value against system value explicitly

## When To Use
Use this before tuning notifications, prompts, recommendations, onboarding, or AI-driven attention flows.

## Failure Mode
Treating the bargain as obvious and skipping the hidden incentive side.
