# Statesman Tool 1: First Principles Deconstruction

> Applied to Kenyan Politics

## Purpose
Applied to Kenyan Politics

## Process
- For any political situation, ask "What is actually being contested?"
- Strip away narrative to find resource/power flows
- Identify whose interests are served by different outcomes
- Make decisions based on first principles, not received wisdom
- Surface narrative: "BBI referendum is about constitutional reform"
- First principles: "BBI is about succession positioning and expanding appointment power"
- Strategic response: Analyze based on power dynamics, not constitutional arguments
