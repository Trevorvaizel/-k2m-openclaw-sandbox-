# Statesman Tool 3: Inversion

> Applied to Kenyan Politics

## Purpose
Applied to Kenyan Politics

## Process
- Instead of "How do I succeed?" ask "How have similar politicians failed?"
- Study every political downfall relevant to your position
- Map their failure patterns
- Build avoidance strategies
- Boyd's Inversion List:
- Young politicians who burned out (over-promising, under-delivering)
- Politicians who lost rural bases (Nairobi absorption)
- Politicians who lost integrity reputation (proximity to corruption)
- Politicians who lost differentiation (became "just another politician")
