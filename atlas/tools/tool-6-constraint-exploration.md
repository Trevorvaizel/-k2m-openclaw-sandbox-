# Tool 6: Constraint Exploration

> *Remove your favorite tools. Force results with what remains. What still works = the principles.*

## The Process
1. Remove your favorite tools/techniques
2. Force yourself to achieve results with what remains
3. Notice what becomes **impossible**
4. Notice what **still works**
5. The things that still work = the principles

## The Result
You discover the [[asymmetric-leverage|leverage points]]. You stop relying on crutches. You understand what actually matters.

## Force Connections
- Direct application of [[constraint-catalyst|Force 3]]: using constraints deliberately
- Reveals [[asymmetric-leverage|Force 1]]: the principles that survive constraint removal are leverage points
- Tests [[context-spectrum|Force 6]]: can you perform in a different context?
