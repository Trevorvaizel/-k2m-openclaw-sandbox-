# Trust Audit

> Ask what promise the opening made and whether the ending earned it.

## Purpose
Use the trust audit to test whether a story is building or betraying its contract with the audience.

## Process
Ask of the opening:
1. What promise did I make?
2. What kind of story does the audience now think this is?
3. What emotional check am I asking them to cash later?

Ask of the ending:
1. Did I earn it?
2. Did I deepen the contract or betray it?
3. Did I surprise inside the promise or outside it?

## Result
- narrative betrayal becomes more legible
- endings are judged against the contract they inherit
- manipulative surprise gets separated from earned surprise

## When To Use
Use this when an ending lands as technically surprising but emotionally wrong.

## Failure Mode
Treating trust as a vibe rather than a sequence of promises and payments.
