# Statesman Tool 4: The Lattice Method

> Applied to Political Relationship Building

## Purpose
Applied to Political Relationship Building

## Process
- Every new relationship must connect to 3+ existing relationships
- Every new ally must strengthen your existing alliance structure
- Never add isolated relationships - they have no structural value
- Density of connections > number of connections
- How do they connect to your Kasipul base?
- How do they connect to your Gen Z positioning?
- How do they connect to your party relationships?
- If they don't connect to at least 2, the relationship has limited value

- The result should match the source unit's stated payoff or evidence pattern.
