# Live Question Chart

> If the line of questions goes dead, the narrative engine is dying.

## Purpose
Use the live question chart to track whether each scene, chapter, episode, or segment carries forward a question with consequence.

## Process
For each unit, write:
1. What question enters alive here?
2. What answer is delayed?
3. What new question replaces the one just answered?

## Result
- dead stretches become easier to spot
- curiosity gets designed rather than hoped for
- narrative continuity becomes visible at the question level

## When To Use
Use this when a draft feels inert even though information is technically present.

## Failure Mode
Confusing mystery accumulation with question quality. A living question must matter, not merely exist.
