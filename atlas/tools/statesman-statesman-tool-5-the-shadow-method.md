# Statesman Tool 5: The Shadow Method

> Applied to Political Self-Development

## Purpose
Applied to Political Self-Development

## Process
- Identify politicians who succeeded with the OPPOSITE approach to yours
- Study what they saw that you might be missing
- Your style has blind spots - opposites reveal them
- You are young, progressive, digital-native, outsider-positioned.
- Study politicians who succeeded as: experienced, traditional, relationship-first, insider-positioned.
- What did they see that you might miss? What do they do that you should learn?
