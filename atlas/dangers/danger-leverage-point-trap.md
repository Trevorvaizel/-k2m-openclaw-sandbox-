# Danger 1: Leverage Point Trap

> *Leverage amplifies purpose. If your purpose is wrong, leverage makes it MORE wrong.*

## The Trap
Asymmetric leverage (80/20) is morally neutral. You can optimize for good OR evil. "Efficient genocide" is still leverage â€” applied horribly.

## The Example
Facebook's engagement algorithm: Optimized for attention (leverage). Result: Destroyed democracy, mental health, society. The force worked. The purpose was wrong.

## The Antidote
Ask: *"What SHOULD I optimize for?"* before asking *"What creates 80% of results?"*
