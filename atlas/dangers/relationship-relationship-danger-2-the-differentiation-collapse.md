# Relationship Danger 2: THE DIFFERENTIATION COLLAPSE

> *Growth without the relationship*

## Definition
*Growth without the relationship*

## Pattern
- Individual differentiation without relational attunement produces a different pathology: one partner grows, gains clarity, becomes more fully themselves â€” and leaves a partner who has not moved. The growth is real. The departure is not necessary. But without explicit relational navigation of the growth, it becomes threatening and isolating.
- The antidote: Differentiation in a marriage must be accompanied by the invitation for both partners to grow. "I am changing" must come with "and I want you to know this change, and I want to know your changes."
