# Danger 2: Certainty Spiral

> *Stage 5 feels like Stage 6. You think you see the territory. You're actually just seeing a BETTER map.*

## The Trap
Stage 5 (Conscious Mastery) feels like Stage 6 (Unconscious Mastery). You've built a sophisticated map and mistake it for the territory.

## The Example
Financial "experts" in 2008: "We've mastered risk." Reality: They'd mastered a specific map of risk. Result: Global economic collapse.

## The Antidote
Periodically return to [[stage-7-the-empty-chair|Stage 7 (Empty Chair)]]. All maps are wrong. Some maps are useful.

> *The better you get, the humbler you must become.*
