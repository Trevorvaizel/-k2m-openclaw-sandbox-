# Intelligence Fatal Error 2: CONFIRMATION ARCHITECTURE

> Every intelligence system gravitates toward confirming the map of its builder.

## Definition
Every intelligence system gravitates toward confirming the map of its builder.

## Pattern
- Sources are recruited who share the builder's worldview. Contradictory signals are explained away. Information that challenges the existing map is discounted as noise.
- The result: a perfect map of a territory that no longer exists.
- The cure: Institutionalize dissent. Reward the person who brings you information you didn't want to hear. Build one channel specifically designed to surface what your main network misses.
