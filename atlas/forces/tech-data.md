# Data Force (Technologist Force 5)

> *The model is not what you designed. The model is what the data made it.*

## The Invisible Mechanism
Everything about a model â€” biases, strengths, failure modes, capability ceiling â€” is a function of its training data.

## Key Expressions

**Self-supervised learning:** The entire internet is labeled training data. Every text corpus is automatically labeled â€” the next token is the label. This is why language models could be trained at unprecedented scale.

**Distribution shift:** Every model is trained on a distribution. When the world changes, behavior changes invisibly until something breaks. COVID-19 was the most dramatic documented distribution shift.

**Train-serve skew:** Small differences between training and serving data processing. The model sees a different distribution at inference time. It degrades silently. **The #1 production killer.**

## The Second-Order Force
Data is the real moat. Organizations with proprietary, high-quality, domain-specific data have structural advantages that algorithmic improvements rarely overcome.

The data wall: leading labs have consumed the quality public internet. The frontier is now synthetic data and licensed proprietary data.

## The Application
> *Know your data better than you know your algorithm. The algorithm is the same for everyone. The data is yours.*
