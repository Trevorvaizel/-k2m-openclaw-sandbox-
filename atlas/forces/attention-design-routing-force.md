# Routing Force

> Attention goes where allocation systems send it.

## Definition
The routing force is the power of search engines, feeds, app stores, marketplaces, education platforms, and AI assistants to decide what enters the user's possibility space before any content-level competition begins.

## Pattern
- visibility is pre-allocated by route
- the route distributes opportunity, legitimacy, and consideration
- actors compete only after the route has admitted them
- routing becomes governance before most people recognize it as governance

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto treats next-video selection, search ranking, featured apps, marketplace seller visibility, and AI answer sequencing as examples of attention routing acting at the margin of reality.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- Which route made this visible?
- Which signals does the route reward?
- What can influence the route?
- What does the route systematically bury?

## Trap or Limitation
Analyzing content alone while ignoring the system that decided it would be seen at all.
