# Asymmetric Leverage (Force 1)

> *In EVERY field, a tiny set of actions drive almost all results. The 80/20 rule is actually 99/1.*

## The Invisible Pattern

- Novices spread effort evenly across everything
- Intermediates work harder on more things
- **Masters ONLY work on the leverage points**

**The force:** Most activity is waste. Most learning is noise.

## What Leverage Points Look Like

- 3 concepts that explain 90% of the field
- 5 mistakes that cause 95% of failures
- 2 skills that accelerate learning 10x
- 1 perspective shift that transforms understanding

## The Diagnostic Question

> *"What 3 things, if I focused on them exclusively, would produce 90% of my results?"*

If you can't answer this for your current domain, you're operating at [[mechanics-layer|Layer 2]] or below.
