# Specificity And Compression Force

> The audience believes what is concrete and participates where the storyteller leaves room.

## Definition
The specificity-and-compression force is the pairing of vivid concrete detail with enough omission for the audience to complete the meaning internally.

## Pattern
- specificity makes the world inhabitable
- compression keeps the audience co-creating rather than passively receiving
- generic detail weakens belief
- over-explaining collapses participation

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto says specificity makes the world inhabitable while compression makes the audience finish the work inside their own mind.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- What concrete detail makes this world believable?
- What is left unsaid for the audience to complete?
- Is the story explaining what should be inferred?
- Where does abstraction flatten lived texture?

## Trap or Limitation
Confusing vagueness with compression. Compression works because the omitted material is still implied by precise signal.
