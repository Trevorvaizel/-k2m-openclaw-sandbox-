# Statesman Force 1: ASYMMETRIC LEVERAGE

> The 80/20 Rule That Is Actually 99/1

## Definition
The 80/20 Rule That Is Actually 99/1

## Pattern
- In EVERY political career, there are a tiny set of actions that drive almost all outcomes.
- 3 relationships determine 90% of your political capital
- 5 decisions shape 95% of your legacy
- 2 perception battles define your entire political identity
- 1 constituency mastery creates your unassailable base
- Novice politicians spread effort evenly across all constituencies, all events, all issues
- Intermediate politicians work harder on more things
- Master statesmen ONLY invest in leverage points
- The force is: Most political activity is noise. Most coalition-building is waste.
