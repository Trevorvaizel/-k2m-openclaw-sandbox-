# Identity-Loyalty (Intelligence Force 7)

> *The most durable intelligence relationships are built on shared identity, not compensation. People take personal risk because telling you is consistent with who they believe themselves to be.*

## The Visible Sign
Someone takes personal risk to share something with you.

## The Invisible Truth
They are not doing it for money or favor. They are doing it because **telling you is an act of their own integrity**.

## The Practitioner's Question
> *Have I given this person a story in which sharing with me is an act of their own integrity?*

## Cross-Domain Manifestations

### In Organizations
The person who tells you hard truths is not doing you a favor. They are being consistent with their self-image as someone who tells hard truths. **Protect that identity in them. Reward it visibly.**
