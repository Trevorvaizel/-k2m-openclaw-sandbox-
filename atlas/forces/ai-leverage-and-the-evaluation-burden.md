# AI Leverage And The Evaluation Burden

> When production gets cheaper, evaluation becomes the scarce discipline.

## Definition
The ai-leverage-and-the-evaluation-burden force is the shift in product work from prototype scarcity to judgment, thresholds, safety, latency, cost, and ship-readiness evaluation.

## Pattern
- AI lowers construction cost but raises evaluation burden
- demos can outpace trust and ship-readiness
- teams need explicit thresholds or they ship hope dressed as capability
- product people increasingly need to reason about quality beyond feature existence

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto frames the AI demo as the most dishonest moment when it is not backed by evaluation discipline.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- What capability threshold matters here?
- What safety, latency, and cost limits are explicit?
- Who can block shipment?
- Are we evaluating reality or narrating possibility?

## Trap or Limitation
Treating eval language as bureaucracy. The burden exists because vague capability is easy to misread.
