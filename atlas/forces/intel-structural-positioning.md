# Structural Positioning (Intelligence Force 4)

> *The value of a network node is determined by where it sits, not how much it produces. Five people at convergence points outperform fifty at the periphery.*

## The Visible Sign
One source consistently delivers more usable intelligence than ten others.

## The Invisible Truth
They are positioned at a **convergence point** â€” a place where multiple information streams naturally intersect.

## The Convergence Point Archetypes
- The person everyone complains to
- The person who manages access (assistants, gatekeepers, coordinators)
- The person who bridges two otherwise disconnected groups
- The person who has nothing to gain from filtering

## The Practitioner's Question
> *Who sits at the nerve endings of this system?*

## Cross-Domain Manifestations

### In Marketing
Your most engaged followers sit between you and the larger audience. They are convergence points. They carry more signal than your analytics dashboard.

### In Organizations
The highest-value intelligence usually comes from the person who seems least important. They hear everything because no one guards their speech around them.

### In AI
Feature importance is a form of structural positioning. The feature at the convergence of multiple decision paths carries more information than isolated features.

## The Master Move
> *Build for position. The volume will follow.*
