# Informal Channel (Intelligence Force 5)

> *Formal reporting systems carry less truth than informal ones. The real meeting happens before the real meeting.*

## The Visible Sign
Decisions are made before the meeting where they are supposedly discussed.

## The Invisible Truth
Formal channels produce **acceptable information**. Informal channels produce **actual information**. Lunch conversations, hallway exchanges, text threads, after-meeting parking lot conversations â€” these carry the signal that formal channels sanitize out.

## The Practitioner's Question
> *Am I building access to informal channels â€” or only formal ones?*

## The Warning
> *Leaders who only access formal channels live in a curated reality. Their intelligence is a performance, not a map.*

## Cross-Domain Manifestations

### In Marketing
DMs, reply threads, community conversations carry more signal than analytics. Analytics tell you what happened. Informal channels tell you why.

### In AI
The informal channel in an ML team is the whiteboard conversation, the Slack thread, the pair programming session. The formal channel (the paper, the model card) sanitizes the messy reality of what was actually tried and why.
