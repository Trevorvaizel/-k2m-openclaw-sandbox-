# Medium And Participation Force

> Old story forces persist, but the geometry changes with the medium.

## Definition
The medium-and-participation force is the way a medium changes how audiences receive, pace, replay, branch, comment on, or co-create story without abolishing the older narrative forces beneath it.

## Pattern
- binge, pause, replay, scroll, click, and remix change narrative timing
- some forms reward serial suspense while others reward density or reflection
- story logic and medium logic can reinforce or distort each other
- participation changes the surface geometry, not the existence of deeper forces

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto treats transmedia, game narrative, podcast serials, social confessionals, and AI-assisted character systems as evidence that new media reshape delivery without replacing the older underlying forces.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- What belongs to the story and what belongs only to the medium?
- How does the audience actually consume and re-enter this narrative?
- Does this medium deepen participation or merely fragment attention?
- What force becomes stronger or weaker because of the format?

## Trap or Limitation
Mistaking medium novelty for narrative depth. A new interface does not rescue a weak question, weak trust, or weak tension.
