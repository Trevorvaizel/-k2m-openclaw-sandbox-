# Fate Alignment (Intelligence Force 1)

> *People share information with those they believe their future is tied to. Loyalty is downstream of fate alignment, not friendship.*

## The Visible Sign
Someone tells you things they don't tell others.

## The Invisible Truth
They are not sharing because they trust you. They are sharing because they see you as **part of their future**.

## The Practitioner's Question
> *Does this person believe their success and mine are linked?*

## Cross-Domain Manifestations

### In Marketing
Your most engaged audience members believe you are part of their transformation. They share your content not because they like you â€” but because sharing you is an act of **becoming who they want to be**.

### In Leadership
Team members who see their career trajectory tied to yours share the truth. Those who see you as temporary filter everything.

### In AI/ML
Teams aligned on a shared research vision share negative results freely. Teams competing for credit hide them. Fate alignment determines information flow in research organizations.

## The Master Move
Before seeking information, create genuine fate alignment. Make the other person's success intertwined with yours â€” through shared projects, shared risk, shared identity.
