# Monetization Truth

> Pricing and packaging are part of the product.

## Definition
The monetization-truth force is the discipline of facing what value unit is actually being sold, who pays for which use case, and whether the business is monetizing the same story as the product creates.

## Pattern
- bad pricing often reveals fuzzy product thinking
- usage can grow while attracting the wrong customers or capturing the wrong value
- products that avoid monetization design are hiding from economic truth
- AI cost structures make monetization architecture even more product-critical

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto frames pricing and packaging as early product architecture choices rather than post-product business decoration.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- What value unit is really being sold?
- Which customer subsidizes which behavior?
- Where do costs scale nonlinearly?
- Is the product monetizing the same value it actually creates?

## Trap or Limitation
Using pricing as extraction detached from trust. Monetization truth is economic clarity, not license to overcharge.
