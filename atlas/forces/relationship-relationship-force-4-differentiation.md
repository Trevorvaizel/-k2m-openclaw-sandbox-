> *The Most Invisible War in Every Long Marriage*

- The visible sign: The recurring fight that has no resolution. The emotional gridlock. The feeling that any real honesty threatens the relationship's survival. The relationship where peace is maintained by one or both partners not being fully present.
- The invisible truth: Most of what people call "communication problems" are actually differentiation problems. The fight is not about the budget. It is about whose self gets erased to maintain harmony. The partner who cannot tolerate disagreement without experiencing it as abandonment has a differentiation deficit. The partner who cannot be close without losing themselves has a differentiation deficit of a different kind.
- Schnarch's deepest insight: marriage is a crucible that generates the heat necessary to either break people or forge them into more fully realised selves. The gridlock you experience in a long marriage is not a failure. It is the mechanism working as designed. It is pressure to grow.
