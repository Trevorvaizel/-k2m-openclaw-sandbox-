# Statesman Force 8: ATTENTION ECONOMICS

> Where You Focus Is Who You Become

## Definition
Where You Focus Is Who You Become

## Pattern
- Your political bandwidth is limited (you can only deeply engage 3-5 issues)
- Where you direct that attention determines what patterns you see
- What patterns you see determines what you learn
- What you learn determines who you become politically
- Novice attention: Scattered, reactive, pulled by media cycle and opponent actions
- Intermediate attention: Focused, but on the wrong things (tactics, not principles)
- Master attention: Strategic allocation - 80% on leverage points, 20% on maintenance
- Political mastery is not about doing more.
- Political mastery is about paying attention to less (but the right less).
