# Organizational Alignment Physics

> The best product decision is useless if the organization cannot support it.

## Definition
The organizational-alignment-physics force is the system of incentives, fears, constraints, and trust deficits that determines whether product truth can be acted on inside an organization.

## Pattern
- everyone can agree in principle while decisions still stall or mutate
- product work is social and structural, not purely analytical
- different functions carry different risk horizons and truth fragments
- what looks like stakeholder management is often system physics

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto reframes buy-in problems as deeper questions about which organizational force field makes obvious product truth difficult to act on.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- Which incentive or fear is resisting this decision?
- Which truth does each function carry?
- What trust deficit is making the choice unstable?
- Are we solving the product question or the organizational physics around it?

## Trap or Limitation
Reducing every conflict to politics. Some stalled decisions are stalled because the product truth is still weak.
