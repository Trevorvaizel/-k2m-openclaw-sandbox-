# Proxy Force

> The system becomes what it can measure.

## Definition
The proxy force is the drift that occurs when clicks, watch time, conversions, retention, or engagement metrics become sovereign stand-ins for harder-to-measure human purposes.

## Pattern
- proxies begin as measurement aids
- organizations optimize what is visible in dashboards
- the proxy slowly replaces the real purpose
- good teams can create harmful systems without malicious intent

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto contrasts clicks with comprehension, watch time with learning, conversion with regret, and engagement with civic value, then shows how measurement environments can turn education into retention theater or civic systems into outrage optimization.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- What human purpose does this metric stand in for?
- Where does the proxy diverge from the purpose?
- What improves the metric while harming the person?
- Who can stop optimization when the proxy drifts?

## Trap or Limitation
Treating metrics as neutral. In attention systems, the metric often becomes the moral center whether the team admits it or not.
