# Proxy-Delegation Force

> When agents look for us, attention moves one layer upstream.

## Definition
The proxy-delegation force is the shift in attention competition from the user's direct gaze to the intermediary systems that browse, summarize, compare, recommend, and act on the user's behalf.

## Pattern
- agentic systems become attentional proxies
- suppliers begin designing for the agent's perception as well as the human's
- delegation can reduce cognitive burden and centralize routing power at the same time
- the new contest is for influence over the proxy

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto frames AI agents as systems that can protect attention by filtering complexity, while also becoming private routing authorities with opaque incentives and outsized leverage over what the user never sees.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- What does the agent see before the user sees?
- Which sources can reach the proxy?
- Who can pay to influence the route?
- Can the user inspect and contest the agent's path?

## Trap or Limitation
Assuming delegation is innocence. Manipulation may be reduced, but it may also simply relocate into the proxy layer.
