# Time Compression (Force 2)

> *Time is not the input. Density of connections is the input.*

## The Invisible Pattern

Expertise does NOT accumulate linearly. It compounds in bursts.

- Years 1-2: Linear growth (visible, exciting, deceptive)
- Years 2-5: Plateau (invisible, frustrating, where 90% quit)
- Years 5-10: Exponential jumps (sudden "overnight" mastery)
