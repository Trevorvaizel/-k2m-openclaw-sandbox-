# Relationship Force 2: PERCEIVED FATE ALIGNMENT

> *Loyalty Is Downstream of Shared Trajectory, Not Love*

## Definition
*Loyalty Is Downstream of Shared Trajectory, Not Love*

## Pattern
- What it is: The visceral answer to a silent question that both partners are asking continuously: *Does this person's trajectory rhyme with mine? Are we going somewhere that makes sense together?*
- The visible sign: "We want the same things." The quiet comfort of a long drive in the same direction. The lack of needing to explain your ambitions. The sense that your growth does not threaten theirs.
- The invisible truth: Fate alignment is not about agreement. It is about directionality. - The master move: Name the shared trajectory explicitly. Not the destination (a house, children, retirement) but the *kind of people* you are both trying to become. Fate alignment is not about plans. It is about character direction.
