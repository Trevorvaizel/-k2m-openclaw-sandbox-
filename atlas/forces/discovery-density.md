# Discovery Density

> Decision quality is proportional to the freshness and frequency of contact with reality.

## Definition
The discovery-density force is the operating rhythm by which continuous contact with customers and reality keeps delivery velocity from outrunning learning velocity.

## Pattern
- discovery is not a phase but a recurring operating discipline
- faster prototyping increases the number of assumptions that can be acted on, not the truth of those assumptions
- teams that ship rapidly and miss repeatedly usually have low discovery density
- product trios without contact are guessing with polish

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto frames continuous discovery as the way to narrow the empathy gap between what the team thinks the user experiences and what the user actually experiences.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- How fresh is our contact with reality?
- What assumption are we building on right now?
- Has delivery speed outrun learning speed?
- Who on the trio has recent customer truth in hand?

## Trap or Limitation
Turning discovery into ritual theater. More interviews do not help if they do not change the next decision.
