# Fit As Attractor State

> Product-market fit is not a trophy. It is an attractor state.

## Definition
The fit-as-attractor-state force is the view that fit emerges as pull across product, market, channel, model, and compounding loops rather than as a one-time badge.

## Pattern
- one solved fit can coexist with stagnation elsewhere
- distribution is part of product design, not an afterthought
- some products compound through data, workflow depth, network effects, or growth loops
- rising acquisition cost often signals incomplete fit system, not only weak marketing

## Source-Grounded Example
The manifesto combines PMF, PLG, growth loops, and network-effect thinking into one claim: the product must fit the surrounding system, not only the market slice.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask:
- Where is the pull actually coming from?
- Which fit is weak: product, market, channel, or model?
- What loop lowers the cost of future growth?
- Are we mistaking initial demand for durable fit?

## Trap or Limitation
Declaring fit too early because one segment or one channel briefly works.
