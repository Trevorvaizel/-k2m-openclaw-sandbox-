# Metric 6: BEGINNER'S MIND FREQUENCY

> How often do you return to "I don't know"?

## Definition
How often do you return to "I don't know"?

## Pattern
- Times per month you question a fundamental belief
- Test: How often do you say "I might be wrong about this"?
- Novice: Never (certainty without competence)
- Intermediate: Rarely (defending what they know)
- Advanced: Monthly (periodic questioning)
- Master: Daily (active doubt as practice)
- Beginner's mind paradox (Force 4)
