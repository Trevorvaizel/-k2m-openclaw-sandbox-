# MOC: Product Management Cartography

> Use this map to read product management as judgment under uncertainty: progress, evidence, positioning, value realization, monetization truth, trust sequencing, and organizational coherence.

## How To Use This Map
Start here when a team is shipping, prioritizing, interviewing, planning, or arguing, but the deeper question is whether the product deserves to exist, earns trust, and can repeatedly convert truth into value.

## What This Map Reveals
Product management is not backlog administration. It is the discipline of turning customer truth, market truth, technical possibility, and business economics into a coherent promise-evidence system.
