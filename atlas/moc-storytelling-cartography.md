# MOC: Storytelling Cartography

> Use this map to read story as invisible force architecture: attention, question, tension, trust, participation, and meaning moving through a chosen medium.

## How To Use This Map
Start here when a story is technically competent but not exerting force. Use it to diagnose whether the missing variable is attention, question, tension, trust, specificity, or a mismatch between story logic and medium logic.

## What This Map Reveals
Story works before it is explained. The deeper mechanics are not plot tricks but forces that determine whether an audience opens attention, carries a question, simulates another mind, and experiences a payoff as earned.
