# MOC: Digital Economy

> Use this map to read internet businesses as one coordinated terrain of attention, aggregation, software, rails, ranking, ownership, and automation rather than as isolated niches.

## How To Use This Map
Start here when the question is where value accumulates online, who captures margin, what creates durable bargaining power, or how a digital product becomes infrastructure rather than a feature. Use the force notes to diagnose business structure before optimizing channels or tactics.

## What This Map Reveals
The digital economy is not a stack of unrelated businesses. It is a federation of provinces tied together by shared structural laws: demand aggregation, zero-marginal replication, liquidity, workflow embed, rails, platform dependency, and automation-driven taste scarcity.
