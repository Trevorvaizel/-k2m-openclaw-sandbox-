# Credibility Before Cross-Pollination

## Definition

Credibility before cross-pollination is the K2M sequencing rule that profession-specific, outcome-led cohorts come first so the system can earn trust, examples, and case evidence before mixed-cohort cross-pollination becomes the main differentiator.

## Pattern

The roadmap assumes a phased build:

- start with profession-specific cohorts that solve immediate pains clearly
- use those cohorts to build testimonials, examples, alumni, and case studies
- keep the product architecture ready for mixed cohorts from the start
- introduce cross-pollination in marketing only after credibility exists
- turn mixed-cohort pattern recognition into the later moat

This means cross-pollination is not abandoned. It is sequenced behind proof.

## Diagnostic Use

Ask:

- Do we already have enough trust and examples to lead with the deeper differentiator?
- Is this audience buying immediate outcomes first, or ready for a wider narrative?
- Are we trying to sell the moat before we have earned the right to claim it?

## Trap

The trap is leading with an elegant category story before enough evidence exists to make it believable in the market.
