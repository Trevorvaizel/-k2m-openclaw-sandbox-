# Practice 5: THE EXPLANATION CHALLENGE

> Weekly Practice: Can You Explain It Simply?

## Purpose
Weekly Practice: Can You Explain It Simply?

## Process
- Once per week:
- Pick your most recent insight
- Explain it in 3 ways:
- To a 6-year-old (simple analogies)
- To a peer (technical but clear)
- To an expert (nuanced and precise)
- Record yourself explaining it
- Notice where you stumble or confuse
- Those gaps = what you don't actually understand yet

## Result
- Reveals blind spots immediately
- Accelerates conscious mastery (Stage 5)
