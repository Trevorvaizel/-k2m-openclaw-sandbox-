# Practice 7: THE LATTICE AUDIT

> Weekly Practice: Connect Your Knowledge

## Purpose
Weekly Practice: Connect Your Knowledge

## Process
- Once per week:
- List everything you learned this week
- For each item, ask: "What does this connect to?"
- Draw the connections (literally - paper or digital)
- Notice isolated pieces (no connections)
- Force connections for isolated pieces

## Result
- Makes invisible lattice visible
- Strengthens connections (accelerates future learning)
- Reveals gaps in understanding
