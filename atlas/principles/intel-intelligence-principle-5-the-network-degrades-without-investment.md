# Intelligence Principle 5: THE NETWORK DEGRADES WITHOUT INVESTMENT

> Intelligence relationships are not assets. They are *living systems*.

## Definition
Intelligence relationships are not assets. They are *living systems*.

## Pattern
- A source not maintained loses relevance, loses trust, loses the motivation to share. An informal channel not walked atrophies. A positioned relationship not honored becomes a positioned adversary.
- The maintenance cost of an intelligence network is continuous. It cannot be built and harvested. It must be built, maintained, and built again.
