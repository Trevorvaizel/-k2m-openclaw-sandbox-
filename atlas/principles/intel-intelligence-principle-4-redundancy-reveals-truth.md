# Intelligence Principle 4: REDUNDANCY REVEALS TRUTH

> A single source gives you a report. Two independent sources covering the same territory give you a *calibration opportunity*.

## Definition
A single source gives you a report. Two independent sources covering the same territory give you a *calibration opportunity*.

## Pattern
- Where they agree: high-confidence signal.
- Where they diverge: the divergence is the most interesting intelligence. It reveals either a difference in position, a difference in interest, or a truth that one of them is managing.
- Never rely on a single channel for critical terrain.

## Source-Grounded Example
Where they agree: high-confidence signal.

## Diagnostic / Use
Ask: Where does this source unit change the next diagnostic, decision, or practice?
