# One Kernel Many Doors

## Definition

One kernel many doors is the product strategy that treats K2M's deeper mechanism as stable while allowing different audiences, messages, and vessels to enter through different doors.

## Pattern

The kernel is making invisible structures visible so people can navigate and revise them. The doors can change:

- students enter through post-KCSE uncertainty and AI agency
- office workers enter through misfit, drift, and workplace change
- professionals enter through expiring models and high-trust diagnostic work
- teachers may enter through relevance and classroom judgment
- teams may enter through AI adoption without outsourced thinking
- creators may enter through clarity, positioning, and productized perspective
- founders may enter through path clarity and offer compression
- manifesto readers may enter through domain cartography

The door is audience language. The kernel is the transferable mechanism.

## Door Discipline

Each door still has to obey the native force layer of its territory:

- attention doors must preserve stewardship and friction symmetry
- digital-economy doors must build portable trust assets, not only rented reach
- innovation doors must respect timing windows and optionality
- problem-solving doors must improve representation, not just offer advice
- storytelling doors must preserve the live question and earned trust contract

## Diagnostic Use

Ask:

- Is this a new kernel or a new door?
- What self-verdict does this audience carry?
- What proof object would make the change real for them?
- Which parts of the current Discord vessel should transfer, and which should not?

## Trap

The trap is forcing every audience through the student door. Transfer the kernel, not the surface form.
