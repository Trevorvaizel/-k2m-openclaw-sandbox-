# Attention Design Stage 6: Stewardship Design

> *"Legitimacy is part of the product."*

## State
The practitioner designs exits, reversibility, consent, vulnerable-user safeguards, explainability, and measurement restraint as core architecture rather than as compliance afterthoughts.

## Visible Signs
- builds non-punitive control surfaces and exits
- treats child protections and vulnerable-user safeguards as design decisions
- includes explainability and measurement restraint in the core system
- can build attention systems that survive trust

## Transition Trigger
Ask: If this system had to explain its attention bargain plainly to the affected user, would it still look legitimate?

## Trap
Underbuilding growth, persuasion, or salience out of fear of influence itself.
