# Intelligence Stage 6: TERRAIN CONSCIOUSNESS

> *"I am reading the territory, not the reports."*

## State
*"I am reading the territory, not the reports."*

## Visible Signs
- What's happening: Operating at the level of pattern perception. No longer processing individual data points. Experiencing the social/market/organizational terrain as a living system you can *feel* move. Predictive advantage becomes consistent.
- The signature: Others describe your decisions as intuition. You know it is cartography.
- The transition: Recognizing that your map, however sophisticated, is still not the territory. Returning to beginner's mind.
