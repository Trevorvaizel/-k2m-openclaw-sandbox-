# Digital Economy Stage 7: The Steward

> *"Without stewardship, digital mastery becomes predation with better language."*

## State
The steward asks not only whether a system compounds, but what kind of economy, agency, and dependency structure it is helping normalize.

## Visible Signs
- evaluates whether the system increases agency or dependence
- separates real value creation from behavioral extraction
- asks what social norm, labor pattern, or market relation the design is reinforcing
- treats digital mastery as a moral as well as analytical stage

## Transition Trigger
Ask: If this system scaled everywhere, what kind of digital economy would it make more normal?

## Trap
Using ethical language as brand polish while leaving the underlying extraction logic unchanged.
