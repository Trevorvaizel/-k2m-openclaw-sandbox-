# Digital Economy Stage 5: The Platform Realist

> *"The platform is not neutral. It is a bargaining environment."*

## State
The platform realist sees who controls discovery, where take rates bite, and how policy can change economics overnight.

## Visible Signs
- tracks discovery control and fee structure, not only product quality
- reads app stores, search, marketplaces, and AI interfaces as power surfaces
- stops romanticizing borrowed distribution
- asks what disappears if the platform changes the rules

## Transition Trigger
Ask: What percentage of this model depends on another party's distribution, policy, or ranking surface?

## Trap
Cynicism without strategy. Seeing platform power is not enough; the point is to negotiate with reality and reduce dependency where possible.
