# Growth System Designer

> Product, pricing, onboarding, retention, and distribution are one system.

## Description
At this stage, the practitioner stops separating product from go-to-market and starts seeing activation, retention, expansion, channel fit, and network effects as one connected design problem.

## What Becomes Possible
- designing product and distribution as a fit system
- reading value realization and monetization as one flow
- seeing growth as structural rather than campaign-based

## Blind Spot
Mistaking personal product heroics for the deeper need to redesign the organization that keeps producing decisions.
