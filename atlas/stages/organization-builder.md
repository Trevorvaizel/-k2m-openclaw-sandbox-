# Organization Builder

> The real product is the organization's ability to keep discovering and delivering value.

## Description
At this stage, the unit of leverage shifts from feature portfolio to product operating model. Research systems, decision rituals, product trios, strategy cadence, data systems, and talent calibration become product work.

## What Becomes Possible
- building repeatable product truth instead of isolated wins
- redesigning the operating model around evidence and judgment
- turning organizational physics into a tractable product constraint

## Blind Spot
Treating AI as an add-on rather than as a change in the constraints of the whole field.
