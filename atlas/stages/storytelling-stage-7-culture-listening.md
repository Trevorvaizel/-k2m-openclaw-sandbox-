# Stage 7: Culture Listening

> "The story is arriving through me as much as from me."

## Description
At this stage, the storyteller can articulate what a culture feels but has not yet named. The work carries unusual resonance because the maker is listening to live pressure, not merely projecting craft onto it.

## What Becomes Possible
- naming submerged cultural tension before it becomes cliche
- turning listening into articulated narrative pressure
- making stories feel discovered rather than manufactured

## Blind Spot
Under-crediting the decades of craft that made this level of listening possible.
