# Innovation Stage 5: Category and Trust Architect

> *"The best product can still lose if the market cannot carry the belief."*

## State
The innovator sees adoption as social transport, not just functional performance.

## Visible Signs
- uses narrative discipline and bridge-node strategy
- sequences trust rather than dumping proof all at once
- works with ecosystem partnerships and opinion leaders
- treats category design as part of innovation

## Transition Trigger
Ask: Which trust layer is blocking diffusion, and which cluster must carry the belief next?

## Trap
Using category language without enough integrity or competence to support it.
