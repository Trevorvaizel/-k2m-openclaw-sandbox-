# Digital Economy Stage 6: The Market Cartographer

> *"Surface similarity is not structural similarity."*

## State
The market cartographer can compare provinces without confusing one economic logic for another.

## Visible Signs
- distinguishes SaaS from marketplaces, marketplaces from media, and media from app-store businesses
- sees the same rails and ranking constraints crossing different provinces
- stops treating tactics as portable when the structural generator changed
- compares business models by margin, dependency, liquidity, and embed rather than vibes

## Transition Trigger
Ask: What force changes when I move from this province to the next, and what force remains the same?

## Trap
Over-generalizing from one winning model and then trying to force the whole territory to behave like it.
