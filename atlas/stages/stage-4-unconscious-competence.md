# Stage 4: Unconscious Competence

> *"I just do it. I can't explain how."*

## Characteristics
- Flow state is normal
- Intuition guides decisions
- Performance looks magical to others
- "It just feels right"

## What They Don't See
Why they see what they see. Meta-awareness is missing.

## The Problem
> *Cannot teach it. Cannot explain it. Cannot scale it.*

Makes decisions that work, but doesn't know why. This is the "natural genius" who can't mentor others because they've never made their knowledge explicit.

## Cross-Domain Evidence
- **AI:** The engineer who "just knows" when a model will fail in production but can't write it down as a rule
- **Marketing:** The copywriter whose words convert but can't explain the belief architecture they're activating
- **Intelligence:** The operative who reads terrain perfectly but can't train others to do it
