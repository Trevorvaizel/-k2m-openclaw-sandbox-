# Innovation Stage 6: Portfolio and Institution Builder

> *"The goal is no longer one win. It is a repeatable engine for discovering and industrializing wins."*

## State
The innovator builds environments, portfolios, and institutional shelters that repeatedly generate innovation rather than relying on one lucky product.

## Visible Signs
- builds studios, protected research arms, shared platforms, and capital allocation systems
- designs ambidextrous structures rather than only isolated launches
- treats company-creation machinery as part of the craft
- manages trust across users, regulators, partners, capital, and internal talent

## Transition Trigger
Ask: Are we building one innovation, or an institution that can repeatedly discover and industrialize them?

## Trap
Confusing portfolio sophistication with contact with real demand or real industrial truth.
