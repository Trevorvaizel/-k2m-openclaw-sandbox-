# Intelligence Stage 2: CONSCIOUS BLINDNESS

> *"I know I'm missing things. I don't know what."*

## State
*"I know I'm missing things. I don't know what."*

## Visible Signs
- What's happening: Awareness that your information is incomplete. Beginning to ask *who knows what I don't know?* Still collecting rather than positioning.
- The signature: "I need better sources."
- The transition: Discovering that one positioned source outperforms your entire collection effort.
