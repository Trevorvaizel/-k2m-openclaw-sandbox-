# AI-Native Product Creator

> If construction is cheap, judgment, evaluation, and taste become the bottleneck.

## Description
At this stage, the practitioner uses AI for prototyping, synthesis, explanation, and internal leverage without confusing generation with product judgment. They know which parts can accelerate and which parts must remain slow.

## What Becomes Possible
- moving quickly without collapsing into sloppy product theater
- translating fuzzy AI quality into explicit thresholds
- using AI as leverage while preserving trust, pricing truth, and restraint

## Blind Spot
Overestimating the value of speed when the real unresolved problem is still reality contact or trust.
