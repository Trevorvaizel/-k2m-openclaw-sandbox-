# Stage 5: Diagnostic Mastery

> "I can explain why this works, why it fails, and what force is absent."

## Description
At this stage, the storyteller can diagnose narrative failure in terms of missing or misused force rather than vague taste alone. They produce strong work and teachable frameworks because they can name the structural absence.

## What Becomes Possible
- separating weak execution from weak force design
- teaching narrative structure without collapsing it into formula
- revising with clearer diagnostic language

## Blind Spot
Some abstractions remain culture-bound even when they feel universal.
