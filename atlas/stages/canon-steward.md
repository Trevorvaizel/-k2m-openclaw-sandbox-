# Stage 7: The Canon Steward

> *"The map must remain answerable to the territory."*

## State
The canon steward maintains continuity without turning the canon into closure. The work is to protect against drift and dogma at the same time.

## Visible Signs
- keeps contradiction logs, review rhythms, and renewal paths
- treats new source material as a challenge to the map, not an interruption
- updates canon without collapsing the lattice into novelty chasing
- distinguishes stable kernel from changing doorways

## Transition Trigger
Ask: What review rhythm, contradiction log, or reality test keeps this canon from becoming a shrine?

## Trap
Preserving the wording of the canon while losing answerability to the territory.
