# Stage 8: Narrative Presence

> Presence itself becomes the medium.

## Description
At this stage, story is no longer separable from how the practitioner perceives life, contradiction, pressure, silence, and meaning. Medium matters less than force fluency.

## What Becomes Possible
- working across form without losing narrative power
- perceiving story pressure in ordinary life, not only in artifacts
- transmitting narrative judgment as a way of seeing
