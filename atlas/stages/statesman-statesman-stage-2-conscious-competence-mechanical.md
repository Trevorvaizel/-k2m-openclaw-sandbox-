> "I can do it if I focus hard"

- Follows rules without deep understanding of WHY they work
- Each political situation requires deliberate analysis
- Competent but slow
- What they see: Territory 1-2 (visible + mechanics)
- This is where most politicians stay forever. They never progress because competence feels like mastery.
- Kenya Example: Second-term MPs who've learned parliamentary procedure but don't understand power dynamics
