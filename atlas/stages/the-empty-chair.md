# The Empty Chair

> The best product is not the one that proves the builder right. It is the one that leaves the user more capable, more clear, and less burdened.

## Description
At this stage, ego exits the product loop. The practitioner optimizes for durable user progress and for the organization's ability to keep serving reality after they leave.

## What Becomes Possible
- building for stewardship instead of internal prestige
- treating product trust as an earned standard rather than a growth trick
- designing systems that outlast the individual

## Danger
Moral language without operational rigor. Stewardship still has to cash out in decisions, sequencing, and evidence.
