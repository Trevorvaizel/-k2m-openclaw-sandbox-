# Attention Design Stage 7: Institutional Cartography

> *"Attention systems train people, markets, and publics."*

## State
The practitioner sees cognition, interface, platform, market, law, education, health, civic life, and AI delegation as one connected terrain.

## Visible Signs
- moves between product, policy, education, governance, and institutional trust without losing the map
- sees interfaces as public consequences rather than only private screens
- can reason across commerce, childhood, regulation, and agentic mediation
- returns to humility because the field is too alive for final doctrine

## Transition Trigger
Ask: What kind of person, market, and public is this attention system training into existence?

## Trap
Turning total-field vision into doctrine rather than ongoing institutional responsibility.
