# MOC: Domain Cartography

> Use this map when the job is not merely to understand a field, but to make its hidden structure legible without flattening it into template language.

## How To Use This Map
Start here before drafting a new domain manifesto or operating system. Use the force notes to protect against borrowed causality, the stage notes to diagnose the mapmaker, and the operating-system notes when the work needs to leave the vault and touch a market.

## What This Map Reveals
Domain cartography is the bridge between the universal cartographer grammar and a field's native structure. The method only earns its place when it changes what a practitioner can see, ask, test, or do.
