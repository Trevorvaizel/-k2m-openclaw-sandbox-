# Product Stability Marketing Evolution

## Definition

Product stability marketing evolution is the K2M strategy of keeping the core product architecture stable while changing the audience door, positioning, and message by cohort phase.

## Pattern

Across the roadmap, the stable core stays the same:

- the 8-week curriculum
- the four habits
- the CIS tools
- the artifact requirement
- the Discord cohort structure
- the context and example infrastructure

What changes is the market-facing wrapper:

- the audience being addressed
- the opening pain and promise
- the examples emphasized
- the community story told about why this cohort matters now

This protects product coherence while still letting K2M learn how different markets enter the same kernel.

## Diagnostic Use

Ask:

- Are we changing the product because the market door is unclear?
- What must stay constant so the kernel remains legible across cohorts?
- What should change in message, examples, and proof so the same product lands credibly?

## Trap

The trap is rebuilding the product for every audience and losing coherence, or freezing the message so tightly that the same product cannot enter new markets.
