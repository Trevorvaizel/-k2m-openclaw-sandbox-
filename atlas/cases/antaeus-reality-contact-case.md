# Antaeus Reality Contact Case

> Ideas lose strength when they are lifted away from contact with reality.

## Definition
The Antaeus case is a story for reality contact. Antaeus stays powerful while touching the ground; the mental-model lesson is that ideas stay powerful while tested against the territory.

## Pattern
- A person builds an idea or strategy.
- The idea drifts away from direct feedback.
- Ego, distance, or abstraction protects the idea from correction.
- The idea loses power because it no longer touches reality.

## Source-Grounded Example
The transcript uses Heracles lifting Antaeus off the ground to show why strategic ideas need real-world contact. When ideas are separated from the ground, they lose their power.

## Diagnostic/Use
Ask: "What ground contact is keeping this idea honest?"

Use this case to test whether a lens, map, or strategy has field evidence.

## Trap
Treating the story as a general motivational metaphor. Its atlas value is specific: keep maps and beliefs connected to territory contact.
