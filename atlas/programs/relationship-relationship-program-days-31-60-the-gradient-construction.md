> *"Build Trust at the Right Layer"*

## Definition
*"Build Trust at the Right Layer"*

## Pattern
- Morning: Identify one specific thing you committed to yesterday. Did you follow through? (Layer 3 competence audit.)
- Evening: Identify one moment today when you were consistent whether or not your partner was watching. (Layer 4 integrity practice.)
- Week 5: Map the current trust gradient between you and your partner. What layer is solid? What layer is compromised?
- Week 6: Identify the last significant trust rupture. Which layer was violated? What specific repair does that layer require?
- Week 7: Practice identity-level vulnerability. Share one thing you have not shared because you were uncertain it would be held carefully.
- Week 8: Notice how your partner held it. Was it held at Layer 5? What does that demonstrate about the gradient?
- Check-in (Day 60): Can you name the trust layer you are currently building? Can you name the layer that needs repair?
