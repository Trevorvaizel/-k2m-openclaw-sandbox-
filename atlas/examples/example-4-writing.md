# Example 4: WRITING

> Visible: The published book

## Definition
Visible: The published book

## Pattern
- Mechanics: Grammar, vocabulary, sentence structure
- Dynamics: Voice, rhythm, pacing, reader engagement
- Invisible: Reader psychology, information architecture, emotional resonance, idea synthesis
- Clear thinking + clear writing = 90% of good writing
- Story structure (conflict, stakes, resolution) = 80% of engagement
- Revision is where writing actually happens
- Master clear thinking (outline first) (1 month)
- Master story structure (3 months)
- Context rotation: genres, lengths, audiences, mediums (6 months)
