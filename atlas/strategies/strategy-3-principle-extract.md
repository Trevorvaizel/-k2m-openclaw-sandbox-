# Integration Strategy 2: THE PRINCIPLE EXTRACT

> Pull Principles From Any Experience

## Definition
Pull Principles From Any Experience

## Pattern
- Do ANY activity (watching a movie, playing a game, fixing a sink)
- Ask: "What made this work or fail?"
- Extract the principle (not the technique)
- Ask: "Where else does this principle apply?"
- Integrate into your primary field
- Experience: Watching a thriller movie
- Observation: Tension comes from what's NOT shown
- Principle: Constraints (what you hide) create more impact than what you show
- Application to cooking: "Don't put every ingredient on the plate"
