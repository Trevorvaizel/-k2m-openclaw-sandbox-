# Integration Strategy 3: THE ANTIFRAGILE PORTFOLIO

> Build Skills That Gain from Disorder

## Definition
Build Skills That Gain from Disorder

## Pattern
- The Concept (from Nassim Taleb):
- Fragile: Breaks under stress (eg. glass)
- Robust: Survives stress (eg. stone)
- Antifragile: GAINS from stress (eg. muscle, immune system)
- Primary field: Your moneymaker (eg. programming)
- Secondary field: Contrarian skill (eg. public speaking)
- Tertiary field: Physical skill (eg. martial arts)
- When one field is stressful, others provide fresh perspective
- Cross-pollination of ideas across fields
