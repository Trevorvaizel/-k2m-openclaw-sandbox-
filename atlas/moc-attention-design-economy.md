# MOC: Attention Design Economy

> Use this map to read attention as designed infrastructure: routed, priced, measured, delegated, protected, and governed across products, platforms, commerce, education, and public life.

## How To Use This Map
Start here when the question is not just whether a message or interface worked, but what kind of attention system it created, who paid the cognitive cost, who could bid for visibility, and whether the system deserves trust.

## What This Map Reveals
Attention is no longer just a private mental resource. It is a shaped field where salience, friction, routing, auctions, proxies, context, and agentic delegation determine what becomes noticeable, possible, and legitimate.
