# The Dynamics Layer (Layer 3)

> *What journeymen experience. 30% of expertise. Relationships, timing, trade-offs.*

## Definition

The relationships between elements, timing, trade-offs, and context. Not what a technique does in isolation, but what it does under real conditions.

**Examples:**
- When to use technique A vs technique B
- How changing variable X affects variable Y
- What happens under pressure vs in comfort
- Why rules work in some situations but not others
- The interaction between tools, materials, environment

## Characteristics

- Pattern-based, experiential, situational
- **30% of expertise**
- What experience teaches, what mentors reveal
- **The turning point:** This is where most people plateau or breakthrough
