# The Visible Layer (Layer 1)

> *What everyone sees. 5% of expertise. What novices obsess over.*

## Definition

The artifact, the performance, the output. The tangible, measurable, observable surface of any field.

**Examples across domains:**
- The chef's plated dish
- The architect's building
- The musician's performance
- The surgeon's successful operation
- The teacher's engaged classroom
- The finished product, the portfolio, the award

## Characteristics

- Tangible, measurable, observable
- **5% of the iceberg** â€” the smallest layer
- What novices obsess over
- What gets rewarded publicly
- What gets credited in media

## The Trap

> *"Focusing here creates imitators, not masters."*

People who orient themselves at Layer 1 are perpetually behind â€” always updating their mental map to the latest surface, never acquiring the depth from which the surface grows.
