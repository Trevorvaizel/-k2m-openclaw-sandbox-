## Pattern
- | Force | Diagnostic Question |
- | Belief Gap | What does our ideal client currently believe about what an architect does? What do they need to believe before our actual value makes sense? What is the smallest belief update that moves them one stage closer? |
- | Identity Stake | Who does our client become by choosing us? Is that becoming story visible in our content? When a past client refers us, what identity are they signaling to their network? |
- | Network Architecture | Which nodes in our network are bridges to new clusters? Which project types, geographies, or industries do our referrals never reach? Where is our cluster boundary? |
- | Friction Map | What is the deepest emotional friction our client feels before committing? Is it cost â€” or is it fear of exposure, judgment, permanence, or loss of control? Are we addressing that friction or ignoring it? |
- | Timing Window | What events open a window of readiness for our services? A lease expiration, a capital campaign, a family change, a leadership transition? Are we visible in the right place when those windows open? |
- | Value Realization Gap | How long between first payment and first moment of "this is working"? What early proof do we deliver? What happens to the client's belief about the project during months of no visible progress? |
- | Expansion Loop | Does our completed work generate its own demand? Do people visit our buildings and ask who designed them? Does the experience of working with us turn clients into evangelists â€” or just satisfied customers? |
- | Trust Gradient | What trust layer does our ideal conversion require? What trust layer has our content actually built? Where is the gap? What is the sequence of content that closes it, layer by layer? |
