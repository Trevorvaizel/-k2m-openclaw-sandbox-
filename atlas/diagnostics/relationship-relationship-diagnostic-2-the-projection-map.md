# Relationship Diagnostic 2: THE PROJECTION MAP

> *"Am I responding to who my partner is or who I needed them to be?"*

## Definition
*"Am I responding to who my partner is or who I needed them to be?"*

## Pattern
- Write down the three qualities you most love about your partner and the three qualities that most frustrate you.
- Now ask: Do the qualities I love correspond to what I did not receive in childhood? Do the qualities that frustrate me correspond to the characteristics of the parent who wounded me?
- If yes to both: you are in Imago territory. The love and the frustration are two faces of the same projection. The relationship is doing exactly what it is designed to do â€” offering you the opportunity to complete unfinished developmental work.
- Prescription: When you find yourself in a pattern that repeats and frustrates, ask not "what is wrong with my partner?" but "what is the childhood wound this pattern is activating in me?"
