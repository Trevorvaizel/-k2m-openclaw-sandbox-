## Pattern
- The procedure: For any significant relational wound, identify the trust layer that was violated:
- Was this a Credibility violation? (You were not who you said you were)
- Was this a Competence violation? (You did not do what you committed to)
- Was this an Integrity violation? (You did something differently when you thought I wasn't watching)
- Was this an Identity violation? (You used what I trusted you with against me)
- Prescription: Repair at the violated layer. A Competence Trust violation can only be repaired by sustained demonstration of reliable follow-through â€” not by declarations of love, not by gifts, not by grand gestures. The repair must match the violation.
