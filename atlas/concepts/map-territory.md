# Map-Territory Distinction

> *The map is not the territory. But the right map is the difference between wandering and wayfinding.*

## The Universal Principle

Every model, framework, belief system, and mental representation is a map. It is always an approximation. Confusing the map for the territory is the foundational error across all domains.

## In Mastery
The [[stage-7-the-empty-chair|Empty Chair]] (Stage 7) is the recognition that all maps are approximations. The master holds every map loosely.

## In AI
"All models are wrong, but some are useful." -> See [[tech-interpretive]]. Every ML model is a compressed projection of the data it was trained on. The projection is never the reality.

## In Intelligence
"The map is not the territory. Never confuse confidence in your reading with certainty about the terrain." -> See [[intel-network-mirror]]

## In Marketing
The firm's mental model of "what the client wants" is a map. The client's actual identity need is the territory. [[belief-gap]] = the gap between the firm's map and the client's territory.

## The Diagnostic Question
> *What map am I holding right now? Where might this map be wrong? What would I see if I put it down?*
