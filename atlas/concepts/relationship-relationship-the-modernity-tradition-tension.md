# Relationship: The Modernity-Tradition Tension

## Definition
Modern Kenyan marriages often contain two people who are navigating different thresholds of the transition from traditional gender roles to contemporary partnership models. Neither threshold is wrong. Both operating in the same marriage without being named creates invisible conflict.

## Pattern
- The woman who has been educated to expect partnership finds herself in a marriage where cultural expectation assigns domestic management to her. The man who genuinely wants equality finds himself under family pressure to perform traditional authority. Both people are trying to navigate an identity transition that no one has fully mapped.
