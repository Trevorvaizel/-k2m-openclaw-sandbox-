# Belief Gap

> *The distance between what the audience currently believes and what they need to believe before your value makes sense.*

## The Pattern

Every conversion requires the audience to hold a belief they do not yet hold. The gap between current belief and needed belief is the **belief gap**. All marketing friction lives here.

## In Architecture
Client believes: "I need someone to draw plans."
Firm needs them to believe: "I need someone who can translate how I want to live into spatial decisions."
**The gap is wide and the client doesn't know the deeper service exists.**

Source believes: "I'm just having a conversation."

> *What does my audience currently believe? What do they need to believe? What is the smallest belief update that moves them one step closer?*
