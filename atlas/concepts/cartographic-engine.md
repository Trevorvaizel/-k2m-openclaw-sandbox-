# Cartographic Engine

## Definition
The cartographic engine is the operating loop that turns observed reality into usable clarity, then returns market and practice feedback to the lattice for revision.

## Pattern
The loop is:
1. recognize terrain
2. observe deeply
3. externalize fragments
4. compare cases
5. collide domains
6. name forces
7. test against reality
8. package clarity
9. publish and learn
10. revise the lattice

## Diagnostic Use
Ask:
- Where in the loop is this work actually stuck?
- Are we failing to see, failing to name, failing to package, or failing to revise?
- What feedback from reality is not making it back into the lattice?

## Trap
Confusing one shape of the engine for the engine itself: content, course, coaching, tool, or product.
