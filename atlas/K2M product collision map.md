# K2M Product Collision Map

## Definition

The K2M product collision map is the synthesis point where the extracted manifesto territories stop being adjacent reading and start acting as product constraints on the K2M kernel, the current Discord vessel, and future audience doors.

## Collision Read

K2M is not merely a cohort, a bot, a curriculum, or a set of prompts. It is a product mechanism that sits at the intersection of:

- [[moc-domain-cartography]] for native audience translation
- [[moc-attention-design-economy]] for attention stewardship and legible choice architecture
- [[moc-digital-economy]] for ownership, ranking exposure, and portable relationship strategy
- [[moc-innovation-cartography]] for wedge design, timing, optionality, and institutional shelter
- [[moc-problem-solving-cartography]] for diagnosis, representation, and intervention architecture
- [[moc-storytelling-cartography]] for question-carry, trust contracts, and cross-medium transport
- [[moc-product-management-cartography]] for promise-evidence integrity, value realization, pricing truth, fit systems, and AI evaluation discipline

## Product Constraints

### 1. Domain-cartography constraint
Each new audience door must be native to that audience's lived causality. K2M cannot scale by translating the student door verbatim into every other market.

### 2. Attention-stewardship constraint
K2M is asking for learner attention under uncertainty. That request must remain legible, reversible, and proportionate. Prompting, notifications, sequencing, and AI mediation cannot rely on coercive friction or hidden routing logic.

### 3. Digital-economy constraint
K2M should treat owned relationships, proof artifacts, and direct trust assets as strategically stronger than rented reach. Platform dependence is acceptable as a vessel, but dangerous as the whole strategy.

### 4. Innovation constraint
K2M should expand through wedges and timing windows, not universal claims made too early. Preserve optionality while the next audience proof is still being purchased, and protect new product logic from premature organizational hardening.

### 5. Problem-solving constraint
K2M should improve representation before it multiplies advice. The product is strongest when it helps a learner reframe, map, and redesign their thinking environment rather than consume more undirected content.

### 6. Storytelling constraint
Every door into K2M is a narrative contract. The opening question, the promise, the bridge identity, and the proof object must align. Expansion across landing pages, Discord, playbooks, facilitation, or future agents should preserve the same underlying story logic.

### 7. Product-management constraint
K2M must keep the distance between promise and evidence small. The current cohort, KIRA, artifacts, parent updates, pricing, and future AI-native surfaces should be judged by progress, value realization, trust sequence, and explicit evaluation discipline.

## Current Product Implications

- The Discord vessel is acceptable only if it carries the kernel without hiding the attentional bargain.
- KIRA should be judged as an attention-routing and representation system, not only as a chat interface.
- The artifact matters because it is a proof object, not merely a recap.
- Parent, facilitator, and cohort structures matter because they are trust and stewardship infrastructure.
- Pricing, packaging, onboarding, and proof moments should be designed as one product system rather than separate growth surfaces.
- AI-native product work should use explicit eval cards before treating model outputs as ship-ready product behavior.
- Future non-student doors should be built as native translations of the kernel, not re-skinned copies of the student narrative.

## Diagnostic Use

Ask:

- Which extracted domain is this feature violating?
- Are we preserving the kernel while changing the door, or merely changing the copy?
- What proof object makes the claimed shift real for this audience?
- Are we building owned trust and relationship assets, or borrowing platform visibility?
- Does this design increase agency, or only increase activity?
- Has the product earned the trust layer it is asking for?
- What is the first undeniable value moment, and how much ambiguity sits before it?

## Trap

The trap is treating the manifestos as inspiration instead of constraint. If the product ignores the extracted force layer, the atlas becomes decoration.
