# Source Memo — The Domain Cartographer's Manifesto

## Source
`docs/cartographer-mastery/canon/manifestos/the-domain-cartographers-manifesto.md`

## Parent Territory
`cartographer-mastery`

## Role
Extension — this manifesto is a new canon artifact at the meta level. It does not derive from the existing cartographer-mastery manifesto sections (which describe universal mastery). It describes the art and discipline of making cartographic artifacts themselves. It synthesizes practitioner research from the vault (Greene, Johansson, Munger/Parrish) and foundational academic sources (Polanyi, Schön, Kolko, Weick, Snowden, Peirce, Alexander, Star/Griesemer, Harley/Wood/MacEachren, Meyer/Land, Lave/Wenger).

## New Knowledge

### Added 2026-04-17 (synthesis from Munger+Parrish + Greene+Johansson research briefs)

**Force XI: The Decommissioning Force** — new force not in original draft. The insight: maps generate cognitive authority proportional to their elegance and construction effort; beautiful maps resist retirement even after they stop explaining reality. Source: Munger ("destroy your best-loved ideas"), Greene (mentor-surpass ritual), Johansson (associative-barrier audit).

**Tool 11: Annual Destruction** — operationalizes Force XI. Institutional practice for map killing: steelman opposition before treating any force as settled. Source: Munger via vault transcripts.

**Tool 12: The Antaeus Check** — operationalizes Reality Contact (Parrish's Antaeus case already in `atlas/cases/antaeus-reality-contact-case.md`). Promotes the case to a quarterly discipline. Source: Parrish / Great Mental Models vault set.

**Tool 13: The Associative Barrier Audit** — operationalizes cross-domain disruption. Forces systematic exposure to fields where your strongest domain assumptions are false. Source: Johansson / `docs/research/johansson-research.md`.

**Practitioner Canon table** added to Research Spine: Munger/Parrish, Greene, Johansson, Parrish with vault-local source paths.

### Added 2026-04-16 (synthesis from Bateson, Argyris, Schön, Korzybski, Kolko, Snowden, Dreyfus, Meadows, Koe research)

**Force XII: The Logical-Level Force** — new force. Bateson's logical types applied to cartography: applying a map calibrated at one logical level to phenomena at another produces paradox, not correctable error. Each level (action / method-revision / premise-revision) has its own causal logic and cannot be addressed by tools from adjacent levels.

**Force XIII: The Map-Duplicity Force** — new force. Argyris's espoused-theory vs. theory-in-use distinction: practitioners hold two maps simultaneously and are typically unaware of the gap. Cartographic research based on self-report surfaces only the espoused map.

**Tool 14: The Ladder Descent** — new practice. Argyris's Ladder of Inference as a backward inference-trace: after any force-naming or diagnosis, trace the data selection, omissions, and assumptions that produced it. Prevents force names from freezing premature theory.

**Tool 15: Hold the Anomaly** — new practice. Schön's reflection-in-action discipline: when a frame fails, resist immediate reframing. Hold the anomaly long enough for the next frame to emerge from territory contact rather than being imported from elsewhere.

**Tool 16: The Structural Differential Audit** — new practice. Korzybski's structural similarity test: what events is this map abstracted from, what did the abstraction retain, and what did it leave out? A map that cannot survive this audit is structurally wrong, not just incomplete.

**Braided Research Lineage enriched** — added: Bateson (logical types), Argyris/Schön (double-loop learning), Korzybski (general semantics / structural similarity), Kolko (design synthesis protocol), Snowden (Cynefin / domain-before-method), Dreyfus (skill stages and situational perception).

**Research Spine enriched** — added source rows for all new figures with verified URLs. Korzybski structural similarity quote location confirmed: *Science and Sanity*, Part II, Chapter 4.

**Part VIII (Frontier) enriched** — added Koe 2026 AI angle: by 2026 even intuition-model practitioners are offloading production to AI, making the cartographic lattice the only non-substitutable layer.
