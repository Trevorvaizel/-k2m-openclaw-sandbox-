# Discord Cohort Ops Surface

## Definition

The Discord cohort ops surface is the operational shell that turns the playbook into a live social environment: channels, SOPs, diagnostics, sheets, and facilitator routines that keep the cohort running week by week.

## Pattern

Its broad shape includes:

- server and channel architecture
- setup and moderation procedures
- diagnostic intake and spreadsheet support
- manual SOPs for the places automation is not enough
- a visible split between private process and public showcase

This is where the playbook stops being only curriculum and becomes an actual operating environment.

## Diagnostic Use

Ask:

- Which parts of the cohort depend on Discord as social infrastructure?
- What still requires a facilitator runbook instead of pure automation?
- Where does the vessel help the product versus constrain it?

## Trap

The trap is confusing operational convenience with product truth. Some of this surface belongs to Discord's current implementation, not the kernel itself.
